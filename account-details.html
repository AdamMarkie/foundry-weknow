Car Transfer pick up information

Heathrow Arrivals

When you arrive in the Arrivals Terminal at Heathrow Airport, head straight to the WeKnowLondon welcome desk with your reference number, where your driver will be waiting for you. WeKnow desks are located in all arrivals halls, and you can find their location on our Heathrow Guide.


Other Airports

When you arrive in the Arrivals Terminal your driver will be waiting at the barriers.


Other Locations

If you are being picked up from a hotel or another address, the driver will phone you when he is outside. You will also receive an SMS text and email when your driver has arrived.


Trouble finding your driver

We’re confident you’ll find your driver, but if you are having trouble on the day, our support team are here to help.
If you have arrived at Heathrow, visit the nearest airport information point and ask to be directed to WeKnowLondon. You can show them the Heathrow Guide, and they will direct you.
If you have arrived at other airports, or are being picked up from another location please call our support team on +44 (0)800 310 0442.


Amendments

For any amendments to your booking, or if you need to cancel your order please contact bookings@weknowgroup.com or call +44 (0) 20 0817 2400 quoting your order number. Read our Cancellations and Refunds Policy
