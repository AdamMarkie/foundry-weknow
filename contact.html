We take our commitment to our customers seriously. Whether you’ve got questions about a booking or queries about London, please do not hesitate to freephone, email or visit us at our desks in Heathrow Airport upon arrival in the UK.

Terminal 4

We have two desks in Terminal 4. T4A - If you have large suitcases, turn right upon exiting to Arrivals. Continue towards the car rental desk and toilets, the WeKnow desk is next to Excess Baggage. T4B - If you have smaller bags, you can exit left through the barriers and our desk is ahead of you, just in front Boots and the exit to the trains to London and Heathrow Express.
