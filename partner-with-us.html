Our mission

Here at WeKnow, we have one mission: to be a friend in the city, delivering personally tailored, authentic experiences with sustainability at our core. We’re rooted in our values and centred around relationships, with both our partners and our customers.

Travelling can be stressful, whether for work or pleasure. We book online, and then there’s no-one there to help on the other side. Now that’s changing. WeKnow are here to make city trips special for people around the world, starting with London. Personal, hassle-free, exciting.

We operate Heathrow’s travel services and information desks at Arrivals in every terminal, welcoming guests to London personally and connecting shops, businesses, transport and experiences to be there for our customers easily and all in one place. We never rest in our mission to deliver excellent customer service, and our call centre is available 24/7, so whether our customers need support before, during or after their stay, they can give us a call from any country at any time.
